Each audit serves as a showcase of Gradle Lighthouse's ability to simplify complex architectures:
1. **The Article**: "Auditing [App] Architecture" – analyzing its Health Score and technical debt.
2. **The Graph**: A high-resolution Galaxy Graph showing the "Solar System" of the app.
3. **The Call-to-Action**: "See how your project stacks up. Run `./gradlew lighthouseAudit` today."
